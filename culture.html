Culture & Cuisine

Experience the traditions, flavors, and lifestyle of the Kazakh people.

Kazakh Culture

Kazakhstan has a deeply rooted nomadic heritage. Traditional Kazakh culture includes the use of yurts, horse riding, and eagle hunting. Music and storytelling play a big role, especially through instruments like the dombra. The blend of Islamic, Russian, and Turkic traditions has created a diverse and vibrant culture.

Kazakh Cuisine

  • Beshbarmak – The national dish made with boiled meat and noodles, traditionally eaten by hand.
  • Kazy – A sausage made from horse meat, often served at celebrations.
  • Baursak – Deep-fried dough balls, soft and slightly sweet.
  • Kumis – Fermented mare's milk, a traditional drink.
  • Shashlik – Grilled meat skewers, popular at outdoor gatherings and restaurants.
