About Kazakhstan

Get to know the geography and languages of this vast and beautiful country.

Geography

Kazakhstan is the largest landlocked country in the world, located in Central Asia. It spans from the Caspian Sea in the west to the Altai Mountains in the east. The country is known for its expansive steppe, deserts, lakes, and impressive mountain ranges like the Tian Shan. Its diverse landscapes make it a paradise for nature lovers and adventurers.

Languages

The official state language is Kazakh, which belongs to the Turkic language family. Russian is also widely spoken and serves as an interethnic communication language. Most signs and public documents are bilingual, and both languages are used in education, media, and daily life.
